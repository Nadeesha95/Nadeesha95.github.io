YouTube Subtitle Translator

Extension / Tool

Break language barriers and enhance learning with our AI-powered YouTube Subtitle Translator! Designed for education, this extension goes beyond simple translation—it provides summaries and highlights of subtitles in Sinhala and Tamil, making content more accessible and engaging. Built with advanced AI algorithms, it’s the perfect tool for students, educators, and lifelong learners. Stay tuned—more languages coming soon!
